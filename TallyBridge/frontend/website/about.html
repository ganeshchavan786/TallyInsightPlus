About AppStarter

Building the future of application development, one starter kit at a time

Our Mission

AppStarter was created with a simple goal: to help developers build production-ready applications faster without sacrificing quality or security.

We believe that every developer should have access to enterprise-grade tools and patterns, regardless of their budget or team size. That's why AppStarter is free and open source.

Our framework combines years of industry experience into a single, cohesive starter kit that handles authentication, multi-tenancy, permissions, and more - so you can focus on what makes your application unique.
